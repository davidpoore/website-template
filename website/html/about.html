    • About Me

    Outside of software development, most of my hobbies are related to either music or gaming. I have been a bassist, vocalist, and guitarist for several small projects, and have contributed to multiple music technology projects at Georgia Tech. I also enjoy going to shows, especially at smaller venues, and I love discovering new music and artists.

    In the gaming world, I am primarily interested in turned-based strategy games, such as the Civilization series, and I am also an avid Magic: the Gathering player. I occasionally play tabletop strategy games such as Warmachine and Warhammer 40k.

    Other hobbies I've dabbled in (and continue to dabble in over the years) include homebrewing, game design, poetry & creative writing, and stand-up comedy.
